If you develop multiple Python projects at once then VirtualEnv is an essential tool to make managing packages and keeping conflicts at bay. However, typing source /some/long/path/name/sucks/bin/activate to start your virtual environment gets annoying.

Thus, I created Python Quick Env. Once installed and setup, typing pyenv project1 will start up your VirtualEnv and bring you to the project directory.

Now go enjoy an extra 5 seconds of life everytime you start up a VirtualEnv!
